Ambush (2020)

Wind Ensemble - 4'00" - Grade 3.5 - Alfred Music

Winner of the Association of Concerts Bands Composition Contest

"As indicated by the title, Ambush is a work which describes the fight of war, and recounts a flanking ambush set out by soldiers as they close in on a victory. A fiery chord opens the work and is followed by a strict melody which is expressed through precise articulation. Strong harmonies in the brass, and bold melodies throughout make this piece a fierce statement of war and victory."
