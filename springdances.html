Spring Dances (2017)

Wind Ensemble - 4'45" - Grade 3 - Grand Mesa Music

Winner of the MakeMusic Young Composers Competition

Spring Dances was my first published work for Band, written during my time as a freshman in high school. While my writing style has certainly evolved since then, this work still has a special place in my heart as one of my first successful compositions in the band world.
