Chasing Crimson

Concert Band - 4'20" - Grade 4 - Alfred Music

Crimson, the deep and rich shade of red, is captured and eloquently displayed in this relentless composition, Chasing Crimson. This aggressive composition by Dakota Pederson uses traditional form and melody while utilizing crunchy harmonies and dissonances. From measure one, audiences will be attached to the memorable opening melody that features the power of a strong trombone section.
