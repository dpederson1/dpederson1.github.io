Devious Dance

Concert Band - 3'30" - Grade 4 - Alfred Music

As a composer, I've always found the rhythmic aspect of composition to be the most engaging element for myself when I write. Specifically, a lot of my melodies are highly rhythmic, utilizing one or two notes. This piece uses that same element to create a dance like work with an edge of anger to it. It's got the elements of a dance, but creates a statement that is atypical from what one would expect.
